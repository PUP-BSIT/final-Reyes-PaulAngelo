Exploring the Teaching Profession

What is Teaching?

The job of a teacher is to impart values, knowledge, and skills to students across a range of academic subjects. It entails developing stimulating learning environments, encouraging critical thinking, and supporting students' intellectual and personal growth.

Skills Needed

A person needs a combination of subject-matter understanding, pedagogical skills, and interpersonal abilities to succeed in the teaching profession. A student's learning journey can only be facilitated by having effective communication, empathy, adaptability, and creativity.
