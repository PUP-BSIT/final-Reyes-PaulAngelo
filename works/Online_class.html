Moving classes online during hot weather offers relief from the discomfort and expenses of commuting in scorching temperatures. However, without the structure of traditional classrooms, maintaining focus becomes challenging. To make the most of this convenient learning option, students must remain disciplined and dedicated to their studies. This is especially important considering the potential financial strain caused by transportation costs and other expenses. The shift to online learning during hot weather presents crucial challenges for students, emphasizing the need for strategies to stay focused and productive.
